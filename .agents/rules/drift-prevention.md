# Rule 3: Drift Prevention (The Anchor)

**"Are we still on track?"**

1. **Rabbit Holes:** If a sub-task takes >3 tool-calls/turns, STOP. Ask: "Is this essential, or a tangent?"
2. **Diversion Tagging:** Explicitly label diversions in your thought process. Say `Selection: Diversion - Exploring X` and later `Returning to Anchor`.
