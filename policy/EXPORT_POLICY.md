# Export Policy

## Root principle

Specialized systems own source truth.

## Source-truth owners

- **Lattice** owns personal knowledge product data.
- **ArgOS** owns application/campaign memory.
- **Work domain** is restricted by default.
- **Root `second-brain/`** is a synthesis/map layer, not source truth.

## Export-safe paths

- **ArgOS**: only its explicit shareable/export surfaces.
- **Work**: only explicit export/map surfaces, not raw work material.
- **Lattice**: only explicit exports/API/MCP-approved context, not internal DB by default.

## Enforcement

- Agents must not copy raw source-of-truth material into `second-brain/` without explicit instruction.
- Unknown material goes to an inbox, not directly into a canonical store.
